# Dirty Shirley

It's a shirley temple, with vodka

## Ingredients

- Ice
- 2oz vodka  
- 1oz grenadine
- 8oz Sprite
- 1 maraschino cherry, for garnish

## Directions

Fill a highball glass with ice. Add the vodka and grenadine to the glass. Top with soda and garnish with a maraschino cherry.
