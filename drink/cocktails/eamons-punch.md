# Eamon's Punch

from Eamon Daly

## Ingredients

* Juice of 15 lemons
* Juice of 4 oranges
* 1 pound of powdered sugar
* 1 cup triple sec
* 1 ounce grenadine
* 2 quarts brandy
* 1-2 quarts sparking water to taste

## Directions

Mix all ingredients in large serving vessel.

Optionally add ice cubes made of 50/50 orange juice and water.
